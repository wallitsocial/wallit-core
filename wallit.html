What is wallit?

Traditional social networks today are not geared towards the needs of people who want to create content around their digital creations (or assets). The need of the hour is community and purpose driven social networks where people can connect on things they are passionate about. wallit provides such a platform for NFT creators and collectors - the purpose of wallit is to provide creators and collectors a space where they can promote their digital assets and gain traction. wallit would allow users to create and promote posts about their listed NFTs within the platform; the users will use the platform's native currency Nifty to promote the posts that they create. The users will be able to post from wallit to other social media, by doing so they will gain nifties with each post they make. Based on the likes and impressions on the users' posts, the leaderboards will determine the trending NFTs / creators / collectors - leaderboard rankings will determine nifty winners on a regular basis.

Target Audience

Collectors

Collectors are the people who buy / sell / collect NFTs. They are well informed about the current trends in technology and at the same time appreciate digital art because of its aesthetic and / or monetary value. These people may already own some NFTs or could be looking to make their first purchases. They already have a crypto wallet and have made (or are about to make) purchases on platforms like OpenSea.

Profile

  1. Age Group: 16+ years old
  2. Gender: No Bar
  3. Geography: Can be from anywhere around the world, but we believe initial traction will come from North America and Europe
  4. Economic Standing: People with access to an abundance of money, and freedom to invest this money as they choose

Goals

  1. Digital art collection: This persona could be an avid enthusiast of digital art, and maybe already has artists in mind who they want to follow and buy art from. The art collection may not be limited to aesthetic art, it could also be digital sports collectibles, video game purchase collectibles etc.
  2. “Flexing" the art they own: This persona wants to show the world (at least show their social network) the art pieces they own
  3. Investment: This persona may only have a monetary motivation behind buying NFTs; they want to create (or further fuel) the hype around the art pieces they own so that the value of their art increases and eventually they get as high a return on the re sale of their art
  4. Supporting artists: This persona could have a specific motivation towards supporting / hyping certain artists (in the future this could give rise to a sub-persona of \digital art promoters")
  5. Knowledge gaining about the \next best art / artist": The collectors could also be using the wallit platform to gain knowledge on the latest artists / art pieces
  6. Video game experience / gameplay / aesthetic enhancement or collection: There could be a particular type of collector persona which is only interested in video game nfts - they could be buying / collecting NFTs for specific games (this could lead to a sub-persona for \video game nft collectors")

Frustrations

  1. Lack of a social network where they can post and share the digital art work they own
  2. Difficult to find artists whose art they like and want to buy
  3. Difficult to track latest trending NFTs and artists
  4. Artists currently are fragmented across different marketplaces, collectors find it difficult to track all / any of them in one single place
  5. Currently it's difficult to find the influencers" in the NFT art scene - collectors who are new to the game do not have a place to get more educated about which artists / art pieces are trending
  6. Current NFT marketplaces lack the social aspect of owning / buying any form of art
  7. No dedicated platform to create hype / awareness about arts they own / artists they really like

Artists

Artists are people who create digital art. These art forms could be of any form - digital portraits, audio art, video game collectibles, sports moments etc. They could be using different kinds of digital / physical tools for creating these art forms. These artists could have already listed their arts as NFTs on different NFT marketplaces or could be looking to post their first art forms on the marketplaces (they may or may not have a crypto wallet accordingly).

Profile

  1. Age Group: No Bar
  2. Gender: No Bar
  3. Geography: Can be from anywhere around the world
  4. Occupation: These are people who create digital art / gamers / sports enthusiasts who like to capture live moments from sports events

Goals

  1. Digital art selling: These are creatives and / or creators, they want to sell their digital art forms and earn monetary benefits from their works
  2. Showcase and promote artwork: They want to present their arts and the stories behind them to the world and their followers; they want to promote their artwork as much as possible to get as high returns as possible for their work
  3. Knowledge gaining about where to list their artwork: These artists want to gain knowledge about which are the best places to list their artwork, what are the costs associated with listing arts on different platforms
  4. Follow and support their peers: They want to follow other artists and stay up to date with the latest artworks from their peers
  5. Buying artworks: Some artist may even be interested in buying artworks from other artists, especially video game NFT creators

Frustrations

  1. Lack of a social network where they can post and share the digital art work they have created
  2. Lack of transparency about the available options in terms of marketplaces to list their artwork
  3. Lack of recognition for the artworks they have created, they want to tell the stories behind their work
  4. Inability to create hype around the artworks they have created / own - they want to create a followership which currently they have to do through existing social media platforms which lacks proper audience and mechanisms for outreach (NFT specific)
  5. No clear measure for popularity - no single place to evaluate the value of their portfolio

They have limited or no knowledge about NFTs or the artists who are creating NFTs or the collectors who are purchasing them (and making thousands if not millions out of their investments). Technology Overview

wallit will employ blockchain technologies in a number of ways to help bring the NFT community together and to fuel the economic activities within the platform.

Blockchain

We are evaluating two emergent and promising layer-1 protocols as the underlying blockchain for wallit. One is Flow and the other is Solana.

Flow

Flow is a fast, decentralized, and developer-friendly blockchain, designed to scale without sharding, allowing for massive improvements in speed and throughput while preserving a developer-friendly, ACID-compliant environment and can be coded in Go or JavaScript.

Solana

Solana on the other hand is a fast, secure, and censorship resistant blockchain and offers blazing speed and low fees while requiring no sharding or layer 2 and can be coded in Rust, C, and C++.

Token

The platform will be powered by Nifty token. There will be a max supply of 10 Billion Nifty tokens which will be minted gradually following a transparent token distribution calendar. Nifty tokens can be purchased through the wallet, or on an exchange.

Reward

Top Artists will be continuously rewarded based on how valuable the community perceives their works to be - as measured by upvotes and ranked in leaderboards. The reward mechanism is also linked to the reputation on the platform - with reputation being measured as users' lifetime earnings minus erosion due to downvotes. Users of the platforms will also receive free tokens through various incentives e.g. artists and collectors will receive free tokens when they link new content to the platform, as a reward for contributing to the platform growth.

Spend

Artists, collectors and brands will be able to spend Nifty to promote their brands or specific NFTs, pages or offers via sponsored posts. One of the major hurdles of normalizing cryptocurrency usage is it's volatility, which discourages owners to spend them due to their price appreciation possibility. Since spending Nifties in our platform might result in higher ROI from resulting NFT sales, this will potentially encourage spending instead of just HODLing tokens.

Donate

All users will be able to send/receive tips/donations in Nifty.

Wallet

The platform is targeted to the mass market, it is of the utmost importance that the onboarding and purchasing of Nifty tokens remains a task that can be easily carried out by consumers with zero knowledge or understanding of the blockchain and cryptocurrencies. To facilitate this, users will acquire Nifty tokens using cards or paypal, or using BTC/ETH/FLOW/Nifty deposits. When the users' spend tokens, the app will seamlessly deduct the equivalent amount of Nifty tokens from the wallet. Key functions -

  • Connect wallet
  • Purchase token
  • Deposit BTC/ETH/FLOW/Nifty from a public address
  • Transfer BTC/ETH/FLOW/Nifty to a public address
  • Token conversion
  • Pay paid promotions with Nifty
  • Receive rewards in Nifty
  • Receive donations in Nifty

Security and privacy

The security implications of user accounts, especially wallet and payment details are paramount. The platform's code and INFOSEC process will be formally audited by Certik.
